Our Feedback

What Works

Customers who look like us, happy to work with us, and with who we’re happy to work with, who want long-term relationships, and who have a lot of ideas.

A lot of customers and users all around the world 🌍 : Airbnb, Samsung, Google, Microsoft, IBM, Unicode, IETF…

A software which is more and more used 📈 : top 1 % of Python package downloads, 2M of downloads per month, 55M since its creation.

A growing community, often nice (reporting a lot of awful bugs).

What is Complicated

Little long-term visibility, lot of ups and downs.

Uneven working hours.

The difficulty to reproduce issues on platform we don’t use.

People who think that we have to work freely for them.

What Sets us Apart

Software with 10 years of existence before being marketed, with a lot of users (and so potential customers).

We’re two (and that’s really great) in a cooperative (and that’s useful).

We love specifications, we love page layout, it’s our niche.

A lot of people are interested in free software,
and also in paying people working on it.
